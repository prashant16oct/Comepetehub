DETAILS


All that you need to know about CruzHacks-Hackathon

“CRUZHACKS” is a Hackathon organized by IEEE Bennett University wherein students have to create a social platform for musician, i.e., a fusion of popular music applications and social media with some exciting features. The app should be a highly innovating and a user-friendly destination for budding singers to sing, edit, record easily.

The required features include:

1. A powerful audio recorder, which records songs, sounds, instruments, etc.

2. An audio transpose option

Additionally, you can add some optional features of your choice such as a social platform where the creative minds collaborate to create new music and monetize their work or something like the popular reel feature.

The best app gets rewarded with prize money worth Rs. 10,000 along with an internship opportunity!

Date: 1st-14th February 2021

Terms and Conditions:

1. Open to college students!

2. The team should consist of minimum 2 and maximum 4 students.

3. Students in a team can be from different colleges.
